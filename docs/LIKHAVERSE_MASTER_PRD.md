# LIKHAVERSE MASTER SYSTEM — Story, Premium, AI & Studios

# LIKHAVERSE
## "Write your story. Build your world. Watch it come alive."

Likhaverse is an AI-powered storytelling ecosystem where users can read, write, create characters, design book covers, and experience stories that can evolve into cinematic productions.

The platform has different user roles, access permissions, monetization systems, and AI creation tools.

---

# USER ROLES

There are four main user roles:

1. Reader
2. Author
3. Premium Creator
4. Super Admin / Platform Owner

---

# SUPER ADMIN (Platform Owner)

The Super Admin is the creator and owner of Likhaverse.

The Super Admin can also act as:
- A reader
- A story author
- A premium creator
- A producer of Likhaverse Studios

The Super Admin has unlimited access to all platform features.

## Unlimited Access

The Super Admin can:

- Generate unlimited AI book covers
- Generate unlimited AI characters
- Generate unlimited AI environments
- Generate unlimited AI scenes
- Generate unlimited AI storyboards
- Generate unlimited AI trailers
- Generate unlimited AI films
- Access experimental AI models
- Access all premium features without payment
- Bypass all AI generation limits
- Manage all users
- Edit any story when necessary
- Manage subscriptions
- Manage payments
- View analytics
- Control platform settings

The Super Admin account should never be charged and should never have credit limitations.

System Rule:

IF user.role == SUPER_ADMIN:
    Grant all permissions
    Ignore subscription checks
    Ignore AI credit limits

# STORY STATUS SYSTEM

Every story can have one of three statuses:

## Draft

The story is private.

Features:

- Only the author can view it
- Chapters can be edited freely
- It does not appear in searches

---

## Published

The story is visible to readers.

The author can:

- Continue adding chapters
- Receive views and comments
- Gain followers

---

## Completed

The story has reached its ending.

Requirements:

- All chapters are published
- The final chapter exists
- The author confirms the story is finished

Completed stories receive:

"Completed Story" badge

---

Every story has an access type.
